Why Vietnam?

1Delve into the Culture
It is a destination boasting affordability, incredible nature, intriguing culture and so much more.Try out their conical hat, AO Dai costume, Vietnamese coffee.
2Best AIESEC projects
Vietnam is a country that is still developing and lot of first hand volunteering opportunities are there and start ups are budding, hence you'll get first hand experience in whatever you select.
3Develop Yourself
You might find yourself in a completely different world as it's among the last 5 countries to be socialist. Traveling to a highly developed country with each minute thing taken care of, in exchange for pay is no big deal. But exploring a country like Vietnam will completely change who you are.
